## Why Pay for a VPS with Crypto in the First Place?

There are a few legit reasons people go down the "VPS crypto payment" route:

**Privacy.** Traditional payment methods — credit cards, PayPal — tie your hosting account directly to your real identity. Crypto payments, especially with coins like Monero, break that link. Even Bitcoin, while technically traceable on-chain, offers a meaningful privacy step up over handing your card to a cloud provider.

**No borders.** Crypto doesn't care where you live. If you're in a country where getting a reliable credit card or PayPal account is a hassle, crypto is just... easier.

**You're already in crypto.** Running a DeFi node, a trading bot, a blockchain indexer? You have the coins already. It makes sense to use them for infrastructure costs.

**It's philosophically consistent.** If your project is built on decentralized rails, paying your servers with decentralized currency just makes sense.

The catch is not all "crypto-friendly" VPS providers are created equal. Some accept a dozen coins natively. Some accept only Bitcoin through a clunky third-party gateway. Some say "crypto accepted" and really mean "we have a CoinPayments integration that sometimes works." So let's look at what actually matters.
